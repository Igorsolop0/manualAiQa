## 📖 Research Papers & Articles

### Foundational Papers

#### 1. ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629
**Summary:** Introduces ReAct pattern (Reasoning + Acting)
**Key Insight:** Combining reasoning traces with actions improves performance

---

#### 2. Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903
**Summary:** Chain-of-thought prompting technique
**Key Insight:** Step-by-step reasoning improves complex problem solving

---

#### 3. Toolformer: Language Models Can Teach Themselves to Use Tools
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.04761
**Summary:** Self-supervised tool learning
**Key Insight:** LLMs can learn to use APIs without explicit training

---
